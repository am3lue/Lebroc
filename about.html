About LeBroc Grocery

Our Story

Founded in 2020, LeBroc Grocery started as a small family-run store with a passion for providing the community with fresh, high-quality groceries.


We believe in the importance of fresh food and supporting local farmers and producers. That's why we source our products with care.


Whether you're doing your weekly shopping or looking for a special ingredient, we're here to help.
