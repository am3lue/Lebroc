About Delicious Bites Bakery

Our Story

Founded by Mrs. Priya Sharma in 2020, Delicious Bites started as a small home bakery and grew into Indiranagar's favorite bakery because of our passion for quality and taste.


We use only pure butter, fresh eggs, Belgian chocolate, and real vanilla – no preservatives, no shortcuts.


Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or just your daily bread – we bake it with love.
